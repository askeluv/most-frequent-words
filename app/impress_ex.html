Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA: /mi/.

Etymology 1[edit]

Adjective[edit]

mi (first-person singular possessive of singular, of plural mis)

  1. (before the noun) apocopic form of m’o, my
Usage notes[edit]

The forms mi and mis are only used before and within the noun phrase of the modified noun. In other positions, a form of m’o is used instead.

Son mis libros. Ñ They are my books.
Los libros son m’os. Ñ The books are mine.
